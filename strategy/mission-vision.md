# Mission and Vision

## Purpose

Defines the foundational purpose and aspirational direction of the RWA Tokenization Platform.

> **Wiki**: [Mission-Vision](https://github.com/CUBOPLUS-BTC/tokenization/wiki/Mission-Vision)

---

## Vision

Build the most trustworthy Bitcoin-native tokenization platform for real-world assets, where funding, trading, settlement, and treasury flows are transparent and easy to verify.

## Mission

Enable individuals, communities, and institutions to submit assets, evaluate them, tokenize value, trade safely, and track outcomes with strong custody, security, and documentation standards.
